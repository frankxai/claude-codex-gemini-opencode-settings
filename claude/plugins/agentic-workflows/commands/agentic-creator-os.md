## Department Capabilities

### Content Department

**Capabilities**: Blog posts, social media, video scripts, emails, documentation

**Tools**: Write tool, Grep/Glob research, SEO patterns, brand voice

**Outputs**: MDX blog posts, Markdown scripts, JSON structured content

---

### Development Department

**Capabilities**: Websites, APIs, deployment, bug fixes, infrastructure

**Stack**: Next.js 15, React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind, Vercel

**Tools**: Bash, Write/Edit, Task tool, MCP servers

---

### Design Department

**Capabilities**: Hero images, social assets, brand elements, UI mockups

**Style**: Glassmorphic, cyan/purple gradients, dark theme, premium

**Tools**: Image generation MCP, SVG creation, design tokens

---

### Marketing Department

**Capabilities**: SEO audits, social campaigns, analytics, ad copy

**Channels**: LinkedIn (primary), Twitter/X, YouTube, Email

**Tools**: WebSearch, WebFetch, Grep, Analytics API

---

### Business Department

**Capabilities**: CRM, invoicing, reporting, onboarding, automation

**Systems**: Vercel KV, Resend email, Stripe payments

**Tools**: Database operations, PDF generation, workflow automation

**Traditional**: ChatGPT for writing, Midjourney for images, manual coordination.

**Creator OS**: AI departments that collaborate and execute end-to-end.

**Result**: You become CEO of your AI company, not a tool operator.
